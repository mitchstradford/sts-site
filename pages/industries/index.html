Our approach

Why industry experience matters in CDTFA matters

Auditors know your industry

CDTFA auditors are assigned to industry groups and develop deep expertise in the patterns and vulnerabilities specific to your type of business. Your representative needs to know what they know — and more.

Taxability rules vary by industry

What's taxable in a restaurant is different from what's taxable at a dealership or on a construction site. Applying generic sales tax knowledge to your specific situation produces generic — and often wrong — results.

Audit methodologies are industry-specific

The markup method used for restaurants, the METRC reconciliation used for cannabis, the deal jacket review used for auto dealers — each requires specific knowledge to challenge effectively when the methodology produces unfair results.
