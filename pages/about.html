Our story

Specialists, not generalists

Sales Tax Solutions was built on a single conviction: California businesses deserve representation from someone who focuses exclusively on California sales and use tax — not a generalist who handles sales tax among dozens of other practice areas.

The CDTFA administers one of the most complex sales tax systems in the United States. California's rules on taxability, exemptions, and audit procedures are detailed, frequently changing, and unforgiving of errors. Effective representation requires deep familiarity with CDTFA audit practices, the Office of Tax Appeals process, and the specific industries the agency scrutinizes most closely.

Over more than a decade, we have built that depth — representing businesses across California in audits, appeals, collection matters, refund claims, and compliance proceedings. Our clients range from small retailers and restaurants to manufacturers, distributors, cannabis operators, and technology companies.

Our approach

How we work

CDTFA matters, exclusively

We work exclusively on CDTFA matters. That focus means current, deep knowledge of state procedures, audit practices, and the Office of Tax Appeals — not divided attention across multiple jurisdictions.

Early intervention

The best outcomes in CDTFA matters almost always come from acting early. We encourage clients to contact us the moment they receive a notice — before the audit scope is set and before positions are established.

Transparent communication

We explain where you stand, what your options are, and what we recommend — in plain language. We don't create dependency through complexity, and we don't bill you for conversations about your own case.

Honest fee structures

We discuss fees before engagement and we stick to them. Whether it's a flat project fee or hourly billing, you'll know what to expect. Our initial consultation is always free.
